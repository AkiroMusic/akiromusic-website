The scarlet wilderness stretches to the horizon; wheel tracks carve winding trails through the crimson ears of grain, like threads weaving hearts together. Beneath the ashen sky, a neon-shattered beam of light pierces the silence. All the world’s clamor is swallowed by the open field, leaving only the soft rustle of wind brushing grass tips—and you, by my side.
The universe is vast beyond measure, yet it holds nothing but you and me. All else fades into nothingness.
